\section*{Brand Identity}
\begin{contentblock}
    \textbf{\color{accent}Feel:} Professional, respectful, and clean. The focus is on the knowledge being shared.
    
    \textbf{\color{accent}Colors:} Use a consistent color palette (e.g., deep purple, gold, white, and black) for all text overlays and branding.
    
    \textbf{\color{accent}Fonts:} Choose a clear, modern, and easy-to-read font for all captions and headlines.
\end{contentblock}

\section*{Reels \& Short-Form Videos}
\begin{contentblock}
    \subsection*{Visual Structure of a Reel}
    This is the core of your content. Every video should look consistent and professional.
    
    \begin{visualexample}
        \textbf{[HEADLINE: The Topic of the Clip]}
        
        \vspace{0.5cm}
        [Video of the scholar speaking fills this area]
        
        \vspace{1cm}
        
        \colorbox{captionbg}{\textcolor{white}{[Dynamic, easy-to-read subtitles appear here as the scholar speaks]}}
        
        \vspace{0.3cm}
        \small Subtle watermark/logo in one corner
    \end{visualexample}
    
    \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
        \item \textbf{\color{accent}Headline:} A compelling title or question at the top to grab attention (e.g., ``The Power of Forgiveness'').
        \item \textbf{\color{accent}Video:} The clip of the scholar speaking. Ensure audio is clear.
        \item \textbf{\color{accent}Subtitles:} Dynamic, word-by-word captions are highly engaging. They must be 100\% accurate.
        \item \textbf{\color{accent}Branding:} A small, non-intrusive logo or your @handle.
        \item \textbf{\color{accent}Length:} 30-90 seconds. Focus on one single, powerful point per video.
    \end{itemize}
\end{contentblock}

\section*{Static Feed Posts (Images \& Carousels)}
\begin{contentblock}
    \subsection*{1. Powerful Quote Cards}
    A shareable image that reinforces a key message from a video.
    
    \begin{visualexample}
        \textit{\large ``The strongest among you is the one who controls his anger.''}
        
        \vspace{0.3cm}
        --- Sheikh [Name]
    \end{visualexample}
\end{contentblock}

\begin{contentblock}
    \subsection*{2. ``Key Takeaways'' Carousels}
    Summarize a video clip in a digestible format to encourage saves.
    
    \begin{visualexample}
        \textbf{Slide 1:} How to Attain Peace of Mind
        
        \textbf{Slide 2:} 1. The Remembrance of Allah
        
        \textbf{Slide 3:} 2. Trusting in the Divine Decree
        
        \textbf{Slide 4:} 3. Helping Others
        
        \textbf{Slide 5:} Save this post for later!
    \end{visualexample}
\end{contentblock}

\begin{contentblock}

\section*{Instagram Story Highlights Strategy}
Highlights are crucial for organizing your content and helping new followers understand your page. Each highlight should have a custom-designed cover that matches your brand colors.

\begin{contentblock}
    \subsection*{Highlight Categories}
    \begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
        \item \textbf{\color{accent}About Us:} A few slides explaining the mission of your page.
        \item \textbf{\color{accent}Scholars:} Create a dedicated highlight for each main scholar you feature. Add their profile post and all clips featuring them to their highlight.
        \item \textbf{\color{accent}Topics (e.g., ``Salah'', ``Family'', ``Patience''):} Organize your clips by theme. When you post a clip about patience, add it to the ``Patience'' highlight. This creates a valuable, organized library for your followers.
        \item \textbf{\color{accent}Q\&A:} If you run question-and-answer sessions in your stories, save the answers here.
        \item \textbf{\color{accent}Resources:} Provide links to the full lectures, recommended books, or websites for further learning.
    \end{itemize}
\end{contentblock}
