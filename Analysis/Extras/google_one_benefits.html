Google One Subscription Benefits

General Benefits

  • Increased Cloud Storage: Get more storage across Google Drive, Gmail, and Google Photos.
  • Family Sharing: Share your subscription with up to 5 family members.
  • Google Experts: Access to support from Google experts.
  • VPN by Google One: Enhanced online security.
  • Exclusive Member Perks: Discounts on Google Store, hotel credits, and more.
  • Dark Web Monitoring: Get alerts if your personal info is found on the dark web.

Apps and Tools You Can Use with Google One

Your Google One subscription gives you access to several powerful tools and features, especially with the AI Premium plan.

  • Gemini Advanced:

    This gives you access to Google's most powerful AI model. You can chat with it, ask complex questions, get help with writing, and much more. You can access this at gemini.google.com.

  • Gemini in Google Workspace:

    AI is integrated directly into the Google apps you already use:

    • Gmail: Let AI draft or summarize emails for you.
    • Google Docs: Use AI to write, edit, and brainstorm documents.
    • Google Sheets: Get help with formulas and data analysis.
    • Google Slides: Create presentations from a simple text prompt.
  • Enhanced Google Photos Editing:

    Your subscription unlocks advanced editing tools in the Google Photos app, including:

    • Magic Eraser: Remove unwanted objects from your photos.
    • Photo Unblur: Sharpen blurry photos.
    • Portrait Light: Adjust the lighting on faces in portraits.
  • VPN by Google One:

    A Virtual Private Network (VPN) that encrypts your online activity for added security, especially on public Wi-Fi. You can manage this through the Google One app or website.

  • NotebookLM:

    An AI-powered research and writing assistant that helps you make connections and generate ideas from your own documents. You can access this at notebooklm.google.com.
